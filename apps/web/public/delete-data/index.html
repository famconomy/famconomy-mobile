FamConomy Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your privacy. If you created a FamConomy account using Facebook or any other sign-in method and want to delete your personal data, follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to info@famconomy.com using the same email address linked to your FamConomy account.
  2. Include the subject line “Data Deletion Request” and provide your full name plus the email address associated with the account.
  3. If you signed in via Facebook, include your Facebook profile URL or the UID shown in your Facebook profile settings so we can verify ownership.

What happens next

  • We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days.
  • Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days unless legally required to keep specific records.
  • We will notify you by email once the deletion is complete.
